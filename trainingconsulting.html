About TrainingConsulting

TrainingConsulting is the professional training division of Skillhunt, dedicated to building technical capacity in water engineering modeling. Our mission is to transfer knowledge and expertise to the next generation of water resources professionals.

Custom Training: In addition to our standard programs, we offer custom training workshops tailored to your organization's specific needs. We can design training around your projects, data, and technical requirements.
